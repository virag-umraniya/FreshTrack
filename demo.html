How to Use FreshTrack

Discover how FreshTrack can help you reduce food waste, save money, and contribute to a sustainable future. Follow these simple steps to get started.

1

Add Your Products

Start by adding your food items to FreshTrack. You can manually enter details or scan barcodes for quick and accurate input.

2

Track Expiry Dates

FreshTrack automatically tracks the shelf life of your products and updates you in real-time. Stay organized and never miss an expiry date again.

3

Receive Smart Notifications

Get timely reminders about products nearing their expiry. Customize notifications to suit your preferences and take action before it's too late.

4

Reduce Waste & Save Money

Use FreshTrack's insights to optimize your inventory, reduce food waste, and save money. Make the most of your groceries and contribute to a healthier planet.

Ready to make a difference? Start using FreshTrack today!
