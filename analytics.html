Analytics

FreshTrack's analytics tools provide you with powerful insights to optimize your inventory, reduce waste, and make data-driven decisions. Whether you're tracking waste reduction or analyzing sales trends, our intuitive dashboards and charts make it easy to understand your data and take action.

Waste Reduction Tracking

Monitor your progress in reducing food waste with detailed charts and reports. Identify patterns, set goals, and track your success over time.

Sales Insights

Gain a deeper understanding of your sales trends with interactive charts. Analyze product performance, identify top sellers, and optimize your inventory.

Inventory Optimization

Use our advanced analytics to optimize your inventory levels. Reduce overstocking, minimize waste, and ensure you always have the right products in stock.

Custom Alerts

Set up custom alerts for key metrics, such as low stock levels or expiring products. Stay informed and take action before issues arise.

Explore Your Data

Visualize your data with our interactive charts. From waste reduction to sales trends, FreshTrack makes it easy to understand and act on your insights.
